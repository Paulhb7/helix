Before you believe, let's verify together.

An agentic health fact-checker that turns any URL, screenshot, or claim into a sourced verdict — powered by Gemma 4.

The problem

The lie travels faster than the fact-check.

Health misinformation is, per the WHO, one of the leading public-health threats of the 2020s. By the time a viral video is debunked, it has already shaped what millions believe — and the cost is measurable.

318,000+

preventable deaths in the U.S. linked to anti-vaccine content during COVID-19.

Yale School of Public Health · 2022

Who's most exposed

The people most exposed to viral health lies are least likely to open a chatbot.

chatOlder adults on WhatsApp publicRural communities on Facebook music_noteDiaspora groups on TikTok

Why existing tools fall short

Reactive. English-first. They require effort.

Snopes, HealthFeedback, AFP do excellent work — but they need you to come to them, type in English, and read a long article. By then, the lie has already traveled.
